Ayre Hotel Rosellón ₹ 8,880 Just 202 m from Barcelona’s famous Sagrada Familia, this designer hotel offers a rooftop terrace with panoramic views of Gaudís famous basilica. Rooms at the Ayre Hotel Rosellón include flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi.
Hotel Barcelona 1882 ₹ 12,255 Set in Barcelona, 400 m from Sagrada Familia, Hotel Barcelona 1882 has an outdoor pool and a fitness center. The 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom and free WiFi.
Novotel Barcelona City ₹ 7,000 This hotel offers a free gym and a roof terrace with a seasonal outdoor pool and panoramic views of Barcelona. The rooms include free Wi-Fi and flat-screen satellite TV.
